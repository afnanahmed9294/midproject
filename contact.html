About Us

Welcome to Facebook, the world's leading social media platform connecting people worldwide. At Facebook, we are committed to building a global community where people can share and connect with each other.

Founded in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg and his college roommates, Facebook has grown to become an integral part of people's lives, providing a platform for communication, sharing moments, and staying connected with friends and family.

Our mission is to give people the power to build community and bring the world closer together. We strive to create a platform where everyone can express themselves, discover new things, and connect with others in meaningful ways.
